Spider silk

Basic facts

Spider silk is, compared to its weight, six times as strong as steel. This alone is not what makes spider silk so interesting though. The interest around it is mainly given to the combination of its strength combined with its stretchiness take, for instance, kevlar which is used in bulletproof vests. This material is incredibly strong but is about 8 times less stretchy than spider silk.
